MY FAVOURITE BOOK


Naruto is a famous Japanese manga series created by Masashi Kishimoto.
The story is about a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki from the Leaf Village.
He is an orphan who is shunned by his village because a powerful fox spirit is sealed inside him.
Despite the hardships, his ultimate dream is to become the Hokage, the strongest leader of the village.
He trains incredibly hard alongside his teammates Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno under their teacher Kakashi.
The book is filled with epic ninja battles, deep life lessons, and amazing friendships.
It is my absolute favorite manga because Naruto never gives up on his dreams no matter what.
